Message from our CEO

We have these schools for speedy reading over 50 schools all over Japan and a few schools overseas. However, our mission is not to make people read fast from all over the world. Our mission is to provide a mentaly safe environment where people can speak their true mind. When people can live their lives by speaking their true mind, we can create a more peaceful world with their smiles.

Words from Nanae Hirai (平井ナナエ）, Founder of Rakudoku(楽読の創始者）:

When I first encountered speed -reading, I was able to see the powerful results and thought it would help a lot of people. When people can read fast, they can have more time for themselves. People may start liking books even more. On the other hand, I felt it could be hard to stay motivated if you are working all alone with a computer. Instead of making an unrealistic goal to be discouraged, if you could feel that I may be able to achieve my goal and do I could do something, it would serve our purpose. Our speedy reading goal is to read 2-7 times fast by enjoying reading with other students.
