A Service and Lesson to become the Real You

Rakudoku instructors provide lessons for speed reading with students via zoom. Lessons include one-minute assessment, rapid eye movement, breathing technique, interaction with instructors and other students and so on. By taking lessons, students should be able to read 2-7 times faster. In addition, they may notice some changes in their lives such as completing their work much faster, having more time for themselves, feeling calm when normally stressed out, and being able to notice their true feelings, etc.
